A midsummernight dream



    Ingredients:
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Kirschwasser
  • 1 tsp Strawberry liqueur
  • 5 Strawberries
  • Schweppes Russchian

Served in: Collins glass
Instructions:
Mix the strawberrys in a blender. Pour it together with the vodka, kirch and strawberry liqueur over ice in a shaker. Shake well and pour in a highball glass. Fill up with the Russchian water
